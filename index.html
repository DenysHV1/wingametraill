About the Game

Merge Cruise: Mystery Puzzle invites you on an unforgettable voyage where every merge uncovers a new clue or reward. As a passenger on a luxury cruise ship, your goal is to solve intriguing puzzles, unlock hidden secrets, and create the most lavish cruise experience. Combining merging mechanics with a compelling mystery narrative, the game offers endless entertainment for puzzle enthusiasts and adventurers alike

How to Play

Merge Items:

Combine identical objects like luggage, decorations, and maps to create advanced versions.

Unlock New Areas:

Progress through levels to reveal more sections of the cruise ship.

Solve Mysteries:

Collect clues and solve puzzles to uncover hidden secrets

Upgrade Your Ship:

Use merged items to improve your cruise amenities, from cabins to dining halls.

Interact with Characters:

Help guests and crew members with their unique quests to earn rewards.
