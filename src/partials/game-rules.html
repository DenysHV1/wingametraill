How to Play

Merge Items:

Combine identical objects like luggage, decorations, and maps to create advanced versions.

Unlock New Areas:

Progress through levels to reveal more sections of the cruise ship.

Solve Mysteries:

Collect clues and solve puzzles to uncover hidden secrets

Upgrade Your Ship:

Use merged items to improve your cruise amenities, from cabins to dining halls.

Interact with Characters:

Help guests and crew members with their unique quests to earn rewards.
